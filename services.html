What we do

Animal57's web development process has a flexible phased workflow that allows us to focus on what you're looking for and deliver you what you need, on time and on budget.

01 Project discovery

We begin by explaining the process, gathering an understanding of your business model, your workflow and goals. Our purpose is to build a “voice” for your vision, whether it be an iOS app, logo, interactive website or illustration.

02 Conceptualize

  • Learn about your team
  • Ask many questions
  • Research about your industry, market and competition
  • Collaborate w/Dropbox and share notes, images, copy etc.
  • Build blueprints and strategize

03 Design

A design is more than a “pretty visual”. Design is a creative approach to problem solving.

A design incorporates some of the following:

  • Using the voice to guide a visual design
  • A conceptual phase of user flow models and user experience
  • Nomenclature (Naming system) and tone of the website copy
  • Typography (Cool fonts), Color theory, Visual hierarchy
  • Information Architechure
  • Structure & Layout (Wireframes)
  • Visual Aesthetic

04 Develop

As digital craftsmen, we are proud to bring an idea into a digital experience or product.

This phase includes:

  • Swift,HTML5, CSS3, JS, React, Backbone.js and other technologies
  • Content Management Systems (WordPress, Netlify, Forestry, Expression Engine, Shopify or Squarespace)
  • ASP, Objective-C, D3.JS, MySQL integration of data
  • Mobile and Desktop (Responsive Design) Layouts
  • Integration of third-party software i.e. Google Analytics, Forms, Graphical charts
  • Email Administration - Hosting

05 Test

After the site has all the visual elements and copy in place, we will begin testing on a stage.

  • Testing on each page to vaidate that all links are working properly.
  • Examining that each page or app screen’s performance is standardized on all mobile devices and web browsers.
  • Analysis of meta-titles and descriptions to optimize performance and search engine optimization (SEO).

06 LAUNCH!

Deep breath.

After we have thoroughly stress tested the application or website and you are pleased with its performance - it’s go time!

  • A web product is never fully finished and is a fluid process requiring maintenance and adjusting various sections to optimize the user’s experience.
  • The navigation and the user flow will be an important experience and as important as any color or design element.
  • We will ensure that perfect balance between form and function is presented to your users.
  • After the site is live; there will be continuous monitoring of analytics, refining messaging and running user tests for new content and features.
